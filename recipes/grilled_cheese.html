Grilled cheese

Grilled cheese is right recipe if you are in hurry but still you want more than vending machine radioactive stuff

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Butter
  • Bacon

Steps:

  1. Put cheese and bacon on slice of bread
  2. Put butter on the outer side of bread
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat
  4. Cook until golden brown on each side
  5. Serve diagonally, because you will get more food!
