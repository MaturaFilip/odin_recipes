Tomato sauce

True Italian tomato sauce should be always ready in your kitchen cupboard! You can prepare various delicious recipes with this sauce. That's include gnocchi etc.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings and herbs

Steps:

  1. Chop all "chopable" ingredients and put them into pot
  2. Add generous amount of olive oil and herbs
  3. Add cup of water
  4. Wait until all ingredients are soft and it looks like sauce
